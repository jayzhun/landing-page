Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat" Luffy, is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the One Piece manga series, created by Eiichiro Oda. Click the button below to follow him on his journey to find the One Piece!

The Four Yonkos

Shanks

Legendary and Powerful pirate, Captain of the Red-Haired Pirates who was also an apprentice on Gol D Roger's crew

Whitebeard

Also Known as the Strongest Man in the World, Captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and rivals with the Pirate King, Gol D Roger.

Kaido

Also known as "The Strongest Creature". Formerly a crew member of the Rocks Pirates, he leads the Beast Pirates in Wano.

Big Mama

Captain of the Big Mom pirates who consumed the Soru Soru no Mi. She rules over Whole Cake Island with her 85 children.

“The world. What a glorious place! Seek freedom, and it will lie stretched out before your eyes... if the endless dream guides your restless spirit, seize it! Raise your flag and stand tall!”

-Gol D Roger, The Pirate King

Roronoa Zoro

Also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, Vice captain of the strawhats pirates. He is the second member recruited to luffy's crew. He utilizes the Three Sword Style, a swordsmanship style created in his childhood while training in Shimotsuki Village. His dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world, in honor of his deceased childhood friend Kuina

Bounty: 1,111,000,000 Berries

Nami

Navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. Second member to be recruited into the crew. Formerly a member of Arlong Pirates in order to protect her village from Arlong. But joined the Straw Hats after they rebelled against and defeated Arlong. Her dream is to make a map of the entire world

Bounty: 366,000,000 berries

God Usopp

Sniper of the Straw Hat Pirates. Third to join the crew. Usopp is the son of Yasopp, one of the commanders of the Red Haired Pirates. Cowardice personality, but Usopp dreams of becoming a brave warrior of the sea just like his father Yasopp.

Bounty: 500,000,000 berries

Black Leg Sanji

Cook of the Straw Hat Pirates. Fourth to join the crew. Sanji comes from the Vinsmoke Family, making him a former prince of the Germa Kingdom. But he fled the Vinsmokes as a child where he eventually met Zeff who became his father figure. His dream is to find the rumored chef's paradise, All Blue, which is where East, West, North, and South blue meet.

Bounty: 1,032,000,000 berries

Tony Tony Chopper

Doctor of the Straw Hat Pirates. He was the fifth to join the crew. He is a reindeer and a devil fruit user who ate the Hito Hito no Mi, a devil fruit that allows its user to transform into a human hybrid of a human at will. His dream is to one day become a doctor capable of curing any disease and wants to travel all across the world specifically in the hopes of accomplishing this dream

Bounty: 1,000 berries

Nico Robin

Also known as the "Devil Child", she is the sixth to join the crew. She is the archaelogist and ate the Hana Hana no Mi, giving her the power to reproduce her body parts(or entire body) on any surface at will. She is the sole survivor of the destroyed island Ohara and one of the few people in world known to have the ability to read and decipher Poneglyph, a skill considered forbidden and threatening to the world government. Her dream is to find the Rio Poneglyph which tells the true history of the world, including the Void Century.

Bounty: 930,000,000 berries

Franky

Known as "Iron Man", Franky is the shipwright and seventh member to join. He was abandoned by his family as a four-year-old, eventually making his way to Water 7 where he became a member of Tom's Workers. An incident resulted in his body being damaged and requiring him to augment himself into a cyborg. His dream is to create a ship and circumnavigate the world with it.

Bounty: 394,000,000 berries

Brook

Known as "Soul King", Brook is the musician and eighth member to join. He ate the Yomi Yomi no Mi, which allowed him to return to life after death. He can control over his own soul and the soul of others. He was originally a member of the Rumbar Pirates but died and resurrected through his devil fruit. He was reduced to a skeleton because of the amount of time it took his soul to find his body. He was alone for 50 years until meeting the strawhats. His dream is reunite with his old friend, Laboon.

Bounty: 383,000,000 berries

Jinbe

Known as "First Son of the Sea", Jinbe is the helmsman and ninth member to join. He is a whale shark fish-man and master of Fish Man Karate. A former warlord of the sea but resigned during marineford war. His dream is to fulfill his former captain Fisher Tiger's dying wish of coexistence and equality between humans and fish-men.

Bounty: 1,100,000,000 berries
