Monkey D. Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat" Luffy, is a fictional character and the main protagonist of the One Piece manga series, created by Eiichiro Oda. Click the button below to follow him on his journey to find the One Piece!

The Four Yonkos

Shanks

Legendary and Powerful pirate, Captain of the Red-Haired Pirates who was also an apprentice on Gol D Roger's crew

Whitebeard

Also Known as the Strongest Man in the World, Captain of the Whitebeard Pirates and rivals with the Pirate King, Gol D Roger.

Kaido

Also known as "The Strongest Creature". Formerly a crew member of the Rocks Pirates, he leads the Beast Pirates in Wano.

Big Mama

Captain of the Big Mom pirates who consumed the Soru Soru no Mi. She rules over Whole Cake Island with her 85 children.

“The world. What a glorious place! Seek freedom, and it will lie stretched out before your eyes... if the endless dream guides your restless spirit, seize it! Raise your flag and stand tall!”

Roronoa Zoro

Also known as "Pirate Hunter" Zoro, Vice captain of the strawhats pirates. He is the second member recruited to luffy's crew. He utilizes the Three Sword Style, a swordsmanship style created in his childhood while training in Shimotsuki Village. His dream is to become the greatest swordsman in the world, in honor of his deceased childhood friend Kuina

Bounty: 1,111,000,000 Berries

Nami

Navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. Second member to be recruited into the crew. Formerly a member of Arlong Pirates in order to protect her village from Arlong. But joined the Straw Hats after they rebelled against and defeated Arlong. Her dream is to make a map of the entire world
